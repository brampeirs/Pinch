ingredients 
salmon fillets (150g each) 4
butter 4 tbsp
lemons 2
fresh dill 3 tbsp
garlic 2 cloves
olive oil 2 tbsp

instructions
Pat salmon fillets dry and brush with olive oil. Season with salt and pepper.
Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
Grill salmon skin-side down for 4-5 minutes. Flip and cook another 3-4 minutes.
Melt butter with minced garlic, lemon juice, and chopped dill.
Drizzle lemon dill butter over grilled salmon.
